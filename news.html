4 June 2026

THE KINGS TOUR 2026 IS HERE

Apapa Kings FC announces a historic six-match tour running from June through August 2026 — six opponents, six pitches, one standard.

The Kings Tour 2026 is officially underway. Apapa Kings FC will face six opponents across Lagos between June and August, starting with Iganmu Giants on 14 June at GRA Marine Road Pitch.

The tour takes the Kings to Isolo Barracks, Hopebay Stadium, 721 Field, and Abule-Ado Field before wrapping up on 16 August against Rising Stars FC.

This tour is about more than results. It is about building the club identity, establishing presence across the city, and showing Lagos what Apapa Kings FC is made of.
